My Own Expectations...

  • Blog
  • By NumbToWorld
Sometimes my own expectations kill me inside so much that I start hating people who are around me. I really believe in reality but there are things, there are people from whom you really expect so much. There are situations where you want to be alone but you can’t. Expectation is like hoping, that you hope that it will happen but when it doesn’t, it hurts you, it breaks you from inside, and what is left is you, again hoping that you will not expect that thing again. No one is going to change him/herself for you, if there is something which can be change so it’s you, yourself. Its not easy, not so tough. Life has million reasons of crying, for being happy though. Sometimes it happens nothing goes the way like the way you want. So much complaints, so much anger, its look like nothing is around you. You are just alone, alone and alone… it happens most of the time you fell into the dilemma thinking about of the person or a thing all the time and still not getting what to do next. Because you have tried your best, and still yet going against you. There are things which I am scared off. There are some things which I don’t want to be left. A situation in your life comes when you usually don’t understand what to do or what not to do. Its like dilemma. Sometimes you are doing good and everything is smooth and suddenly someone comes to you and gives you an advice. Which is somewhere positive and negative though. But you have to be calm and patient about the happening. Don’t worry about your life comes. You are a great person and you are still very lucky to be able to make everything work. Just think about the fact and go with the flow.
