WE IGNORE...

  • Blog
  • By NumbToWorld

Sometimes you have people around you and you even don’t care or maybe sometimes you don’t give importance to them.

But than when you start talking to them and then you realize that how much a person can mean to you., How much someone can make you feel special.

You never know what’s going on in others mind, until and unless they tell you.

Sometimes you just ignore the people and when you start talking to them and then you realize that you lost the time, which could be precious, the time which could be remembered as memories but you just lost it.

All you can do is wait for the moment, so that you can make some memories together and live it.

Sometimes its not the right time.

And sometimes its not the right person.

But whatever happens, happens for a reason.

People come and People go, all you can do is enjoy the moment.

We ignore people, our classmates with whom we might not talk, our fellas whom we ignore because we don’t like them, all we do is give them a smile and move on.

We should try to communicate; we should have a concern.

Am writing this because I got a really good friend, whom I was not communicating with, but now the vibe match so much and that’s what made me write this. That we ignore the people, we ignore the happiness around us, we guys are so busy in ourselves that we don’t even look for each other.

I know this quarantine sucks, and we all are getting depressed at home but we have to do something.

This quarantine has changed many lives, someone is doing great and someone is falling down.

Our family needs us, our friends need our support. And that’s what we need back.

Life is not a one-way process we all need someone in our lives, we need a support, a moral support and emotional though.

Am somewhere happy about being at home because am getting to know myself better, my family better, and I have met more people and wonderful people during this time, which I think would not have happened ever. And am also hating it because am not able to, meet my people physically, but am glad that I got people who have my back and who are supporting me.

You should also try to communicate to people, maybe they don’t like you, but at least try to be good. Just text people randomly and ask how they are doing; it is not cringe as it looks like. We think people are texting us because they need something or they are just trying to hit on us, I think this is our mentality, somewhere it is true, but there is no bad in being good.

BE good to People.
