Sometimes you don’t know what to do with your life

And sometimes you yourself don’t want to do.

Sometimes it feels like you have everything, and sometimes you don’t

Life is such a mess or maybe we make it, but still, it’s a mess.

Nobody cares what you are going through, not because they seriously don’t care.

Maybe it’s because they are also going through something.

I am 20, I know what my plans are for my future. And I have seen people who are 25 or something and they don’t even know what to do in their life.

And me having plans, I still get scared sometimes that “what if something doesn’t work out?”

“What if I get fail?”

I know there is a lot in everyone’s life to deal with, because am dealing with my too.

I don’t have any major problems, but still, it feels like that I have some. And why is it so? I myself don’t know.

A tie doesn’t get knotted itself someone must have to knot it and if still it doesn’t look good or perfect to you as per your wish just untie it and try to make it as per your convenience. because you are the one who is going to wear it, you should be comfortable with it. And that’s what life is like you have to see your comfort, and you also have to look at the people around you.

You doing great in your life, all you need is a little rest and comfort.
