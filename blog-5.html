I DON'T GET JEALOUS...

  • Blog
  • By NumbToWorld
I don’t get jealous with others as usual. But whenever I see lovers around me, that makes me feel jealous, being far from each other. And I miss you all the times, we have more feelings than memories. You get disappear now when I am dreaming of your face. I am here without you. But you’re still on my lonely mind I think about you and I dream about you all the time. I am here without you, but you’re still with me in my dreams. The miles just keep rolling it gets hard but it won’t take away my love. There’s only so many songs, that I can sing to pass the time. All I have is this picture in a frame. That I hold close, to see your face every day. With you is where I’d rather be. But we’re stuck where we are. And it’s so hard, that you’re so far. I wish that you were here with me. Now the minutes feel like hours and the hours feel like days. While I’m away. I know right now you can’t be at home but you are coming home soon.
