I don't ask for things I deserve...

  • Blog
  • By NumbToWorld

Am so absurd these days. I don’t think I do ask for things from someone anymore.

I like being alone, I have stopped expecting things from people around me or anyone. Even though I have stopped complaining about the things, even though I deserve, I don’t ask for it.

Am not living up to my will somewhere. if you call me, I will call you back. If you don’t, even though I won’t.

If you give me time, I will be happy and if you don’t, I won’t ask for it. Even though I deserve it, I won’t ask. Because that’s what expecting is, I guess.

If I ask for a thing once, and you give me that, that’s huge. And if you don’t then I wont ask for it again.

I don’t want to have things from people they don’t want to give me without asking. I deserve to have that, and I don’t have to ask for things I deserve.

We all deserve to be happy and we all want to be.

It’s a saying,” Be wild but stay soft”.

And I guess that’s what I am up to these days.

People are following their dreams. And what am I doing?

Living up to someone’s expectations? Expecting someone to give me what I deserve?

No am not that person, I am the person who knows what I want. I am the person who is that confident, that if you don’t treat me well enough, I can make you feel that too. But still, I won’t choose that way, all I can do is stop asking for things, and I will start investing things on myself.

And nothing is wrong in that!

Everyone have their own way of seeing things, and I have too.

I don’t know at what stage of life you are in, But I can assure you that whatever is coming is better than what has gone!

So keep the spirit up, start investing on yourself, stop expecting from people. You know what you deserve, so stop letting people doing things which you don’t expect them to do by doing those things by yourself and stop expecting.

Whatever is written in your way will come to you, all you have to do is wait for it and fight for it.

Don’t ask for the things you deserve……………
